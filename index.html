HOME

This is the main screen of Can or Not.

It helps people shape text into natural Singlish prompts or clearer ChatGPT-ready prompts without extra clutter.

The website is designed as a small retro system where each section feels contained and easy to explore.

HOW IT WORKS

You type your original idea or question into the input box.

The left side gives a natural Singaporean Singlish version.

The right side either answers your question directly or turns rough wording into clear standard English.

It also detects time-sensitive questions like current leaders, news, prices, weather, schedules, and latest questions, then offers a CoThink check.

EXAMPLES

Example one: turn a plain sentence into a more local and human Singlish phrasing.

Example two: answer a direct question clearly in standard English.

The point is not only rewriting, but helping the user get either a better prompt or a real answer when the question is clear enough.

ABOUT

Can or Not is a lightweight AI writing and answering tool with local flavour.

It was built to make interaction with AI clearer, more direct, and more useful for everyday people.

The project combines retro interface design, practical answering, prompt shaping, and a simple human-first workflow.
